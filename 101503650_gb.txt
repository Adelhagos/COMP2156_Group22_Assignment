GBC operates multiple campuses in downtown Toronto, including the St. James, Casa Loma, and Waterfront campuses.
The college offers over 170 full-time programs across various fields, including:
Arts, Design & Information Technology: Honours Bachelor of Brand Design, Honours Bachelor of Digital Experience.
Business: Honours Bachelor of Commerce (Financial Services), Honours Bachelor of Business Administration (Business Analytics).
The college actively celebrates cultural events, such as Black Futures Month, to recognize and honor the achievements of Black communities.
